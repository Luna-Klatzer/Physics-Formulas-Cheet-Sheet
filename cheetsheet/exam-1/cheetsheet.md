# Cheetsheet for the 1. exam of the 4th grade

*Only in German!*

## Fadenpendel

### Periodendauer

$T ... Periodendauer({[T]} = s)$

$l ... Fadenlänge({[l] = m})$

$g ... Erdbeschleunigung({[g] = {m \over s²}}$)

$T = {2 \pi \sqrt{l \over g}}$

### Frequenz

$f ... Frequenz ({[f] = {1 \over s}} = Hz$)

$f = {1 \over T}$

### Winkelgeschwindigkeit

$ω ... Winkelgeschwindigkeit ({[ω] = {2 \pi \over T}})$

$ω = {2 \pi \over T}$

$ω = {2 \pi f}$
